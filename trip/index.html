ABOUT

Amsterdam

Amsterdam is the capital and most populous city of the Netherlands.
The capital of the Netherlands, Amsterdam is famous for its coffee shops, red lights, houseboats, historic architecture, and famous canals.
There’s nothing like cruising around the canals on a sunny day or lying in Vondelpark reading a book!

Top attractions in Netherlands

Red Light District

Go past the scenic canals into the narrowest alleys of this famous area. Discover the secrets of the women behind the red lit windows.

Walking Tour in Dutch

Walking tour through the center of Amsterdam and see numerous historic buildings and the most important squares in the city.

Rotterdam Walking Tour

Discover the magnificent architecture and hear the best historical stories.The walking tour is led by a passionate local tour guide.

Van Gogh Museum

Discover masterworks by the great artist at the Van Gogh Museum and cruise past beautiful bridges and merchant houses.

The Story of Anne Frank

Take a walk through Amsterdam with a professional guide who will tell you about the city during WWII, through the eyes of Anne Frank.
